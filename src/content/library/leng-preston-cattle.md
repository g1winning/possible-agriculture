The headline numbers from this trial are the strongest evidence for biochar as a cattle feed additive. The trial used rice-husk biochar at 900°C — a different feedstock from Possible Agriculture Biochar, but the same fundamental high-temperature property profile, so we expect the mechanism transfers.

The 25% live-weight gain at just 0.6% dietary inclusion is the kind of number that makes a feedlot economics conversation interesting. The methane reductions are bonus territory for any operator already thinking about emissions reporting.
