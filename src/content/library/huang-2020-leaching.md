This is the source for the leaching-reduction table we reproduce on the Biochar page. Practical implication: at fertiliser-application rates that operators are already familiar with, biochar can hold onto a meaningful percentage of the nitrogen and phosphorus that would otherwise leach away.

The 8% inclusion rate that optimises N control is on the high end for a paddock-wide amendment — but it's well within reach for high-value crops, sports grounds, parklands, or compost-windrow integration.
