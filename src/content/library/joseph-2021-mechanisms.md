This 34-page review is the most authoritative source we cite on biochar's soil and plant responses. The author list reads like a who's who of the biochar research world, and the paper covers both the mechanisms that produce the benefits and the conditions under which biochar <em>doesn't</em> work — which matters as much as the positive findings.

The 12–50% non-CO₂ GHG reduction figure (N₂O and CH₄) is the corrected source for what older Possible Ag materials labelled as "VOC reduction" — a misattribution that's been fixed across our current copy.
