If you're considering on-farm biochar production, read this alongside our YouTube channel. Kelpie Wilson has been one of the loudest practical voices on flame-curtain biochar production for years, and her work is closely aligned with the approach we use on Australian farms.

The 70–80% biomass weight reduction figure you'll hear quoted in biochar conversations comes out of work like this — it's why a shelterbelt that takes a day's work to clear becomes a manageable volume of biochar that fits on a single pallet.
