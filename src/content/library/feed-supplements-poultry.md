The cattle research gets the headlines, but biochar works as a feed additive across multiple species — and the application rates are different for each. This paper is the source we use for poultry, pig, and sheep inclusion guidance.

Practical implication: if you've got a mixed operation, biochar in the feed can do work for you across all your stock classes, not just the cattle.
