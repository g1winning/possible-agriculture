The composting entry point for any poultry operator. Litter is a high-nitrogen, high-odour, high-phosphorus stream that's usually treated as a disposal problem. Biochar at 10–20% by volume turns the same stream into a stable, valuable compost — one that holds onto its nutrients on the way to the paddock rather than losing them to the atmosphere and the groundwater.

The bulking-agent function also speeds the composting cycle and improves the structural quality of the finished product, which matters if you're spreading it through standard ag equipment.
