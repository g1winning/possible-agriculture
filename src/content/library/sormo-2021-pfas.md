The most-cited recent paper on biochar as a PFAS-remediation option. The team tested activated biochar at 5% inclusion in low-organic-matter contaminated soils and recorded leaching reductions of 98–100% — effectively complete stabilisation. Performance falls off in soils with high organic-matter content, so site-specific pilot trials are essential before any full-scale application.

For Possible Agriculture Biochar this is doubly relevant because the product profile (high-temperature pyrolysis, hardwood feedstock, rainwater activation) is exactly the kind described in the paper as most effective.
